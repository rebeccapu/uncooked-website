What happens next
1
We review your submission
Every founder submission gets read by the team. We look at the problem, the research connection, and whether there's a researcher who could add something real to the conversation.
2
We find the right researcher to pair you with
If you're a fit, we identify the researcher who's spent the most time on the underlying science — not just the one with the best name recognition. The pairing has to be substantive.
3
We reach out
If it's a go, we'll email you within two weeks to introduce the pairing and get a recording scheduled. The conversation is informal — more office hours than pitch session.
