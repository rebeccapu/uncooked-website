What happens next
1
We review your submission
Every researcher submission gets read by the team. We look at the research, the sector fit, and whether there's a founder we could pair them with meaningfully.
2
We find the right pairing
If the research is a fit, we identify the founder working on the closest related problem. That's usually the hardest part — and the most important one.
3
We reach out
If it's a go, we'll email you within two weeks to introduce the pairing and schedule a call. If it's not quite right for this episode cycle, we may hold it for a future one.
