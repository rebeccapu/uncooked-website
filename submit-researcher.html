Researcher

Know a researcher whose work belongs on Uncooked? We pair world-leading professors and scientists with founders tackling the same hard problem. Tell us about the work — we'll take it from there.

What we're looking for

Researchers working on the hardest climate problems — water, wildfire, EVs, mining, data centers, cryosphere, energy storage, animal ag, nuclear, geothermal.

The best fits are people whose work has clear real-world implications but hasn't been translated for a practitioner audience yet. Grad students welcome.

We read every submission. If it's a fit, we'll reach out within two weeks.

What happens next
We read everything

Every submission gets reviewed. If the research is a fit, we'll reach out to you and the researcher to set up a call. If it's not quite right for now, we may hold it for a future episode.

The Format
20–25 minutes, informal

We pair the researcher with a founder working on a related problem. The conversation is exploratory, not lecture-y — more office hours than TED talk. We handle all the logistics and editing.
