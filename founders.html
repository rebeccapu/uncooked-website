The People Behind Uncooked

Built by
practitioners,
not fans.

Uncooked was built by four people who work in climate — in risk modeling, sustainable finance, product, and design. We made it because we couldn't find the show we wanted to listen to.

B
Becca Pu
Co-Founder · Co-Host
MBA · Berkeley Haas · S&P Global Climate Risk

Becca lives to funnel big money towards hard climate problems. She's built climate risk models for the World Bank and a climate tech startup, believing that quantifying the negative financial impact of climate change can push companies to make better decisions. She also runs a sustainable fashion brand upcycling ethnic minority textiles in rural China. B.S. Economics & Data Science, UC Berkeley.

What we're actually building
🎙
The Podcast
20–25 minute episodes pairing a world-leading researcher with a founder tackling the same problem. The tension between lab and market is the content.
📬
The Newsletter
Bi-weekly. Lab Watch + The Pairing + Deal Anatomy + Signal vs. Noise. Early signal on what's coming out of labs before it shows up in a pitch deck.
🔗
The Network
A curated community of climate practitioners, founders, researchers, and investors. Starting at Berkeley and Columbia. Expanding across the full research university landscape.
