WaHa / Novnat
Berkeley Spinout
MOF-303 atmospheric water harvesting

Two-thirds of the global population will face water stress by 2050. Prof. Yaghi's metal-organic frameworks can pull drinking water from desert air at 7% humidity using only sunlight. The question is whether it can scale to the billions who need it — and at what cost. That gap between the lab and the real world is exactly what this episode is about.
